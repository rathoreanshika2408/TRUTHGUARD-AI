Suggested questions
Tactic 01
🚨 Urgency triggers
Fake messages create artificial time pressure. Phrases like "Share in 2 hours" or "अभी करें" are classic red flags. Real news doesn't expire in 2 hours — pause and verify first.
Tactic 02
😱 Fear-based messaging
Content designed to scare you bypasses rational thinking. Fake health cures, disease hoaxes, and religious threat messages all weaponize fear to get shares. Always ask: does a verified news source confirm this?
Tactic 03
🏛️ Fake government schemes
India's most dangerous scam category. Real schemes are only announced on pib.gov.in — never through WhatsApp forwards. Never share your Aadhaar, OTP, or bank details from a forward.
